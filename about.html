About Vaultrap

A cybersecurity company with a focused product ambition.

Vaultrap is presented here as a company building MAYA, a cybersecurity product centered on deception-led visibility and stronger operator response. This page gives the brand a more human and founder-led dimension while keeping the tone premium.

Good cybersecurity brands do not only explain their product. They explain the conviction behind building it.

Vaultrap's story is that strong security should improve certainty. MAYA is positioned around giving teams better signal and a more disciplined response path, instead of making their environment noisier and more confusing.
