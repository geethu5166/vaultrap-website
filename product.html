What MAYA does

MAYA turns detection, context, and response into one usable cybersecurity workflow.

MAYA is built to help operators see hostile activity earlier, understand it faster, and move into action with more confidence. Instead of giving the team another disconnected dashboard, MAYA is designed to connect signal, explanation, and response.
