Product page

MAYA is Vaultrap's cybersecurity product.

MAYA is positioned as a deception-led platform that helps defenders create better signal, understand attacker behavior faster, and move into response with more confidence. This page explains the product in language buyers can understand.

The goal is not to sound louder than every other cyber company. The goal is to sound more credible, more useful, and more prepared.

How MAYA works

A simple operational workflow.

Strong cybersecurity products are easier to trust when their workflow is easy to follow. MAYA is now described in a way that feels practical, not theatrical.

Expose

Capture

Explain

Respond

Make it easier for teams to align on next actions and reporting.
